I’m a rising fourth-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at Brown University, where I am advised by [Jeff Huang](https://jeffhuang.com/).

My research focuses on creating personalized systems to support social data-driven self-reflection. By applying computational techniques to social data, my work aims to evaluate, enhance, and build human-centered AI systems to address critical societal challenges. 

My systems have been deployed in healthcare [[1]](https://corona.assisted.pk/en)[[2]](https://autism.assisted.pk/en), education, legal aid for gender-based violence [[3]](https://humqadam.shirkatgah.org/), and for the marginalized population in the Global South.
