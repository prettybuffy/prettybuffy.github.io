I’m a rising fourth-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at Brown University, where I am advised by [Jeff Huang](https://jeffhuang.com/).

I study human-AI collaboration to tackle real-world challenges, with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Human-AI Interaction, and Computational Social Science. My long term research goal is to evaluate, enhance and build human-centered AI systems to address critical societal challenges.
